Online Directory of RISM Library Sigla

RISM assigns an abbreviation, called a library siglum, to institutions worldwide that hold musical sources or material related to the research of music. If you are looking for a siglum that is not yet included in the directory, please [contact](mailto:contact@rism.info) us and we will create a new one.
